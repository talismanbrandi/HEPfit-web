Detailed Description

A class for the binned observable \(<P_6'>\) in \(M \to V l^+l^-\).

Author
HEPfit Collaboration
Copyright
GNU General Public License

This class is used to compute the binned observable \(<P_6'>\) in \(M \to V l^+l^-\) in terms of the binned CP averaged helicity coefficients \(<\Sigma_i>\), computed in the MVll class:

\[ <P_6'>=- \frac {<\Sigma_7>}{2 \sqrt{-<\Sigma_{2s}><\Sigma_{2c}>}}\,. \]
