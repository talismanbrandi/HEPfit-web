Detailed Description

A module for general Two-Higgs-Doublet models (without the assumption of a \(Z_2\) symmetry).

This module contains code necessary for analyzing theoretical and experimental constraints on general symmetric Two-Higgs-Doublet models. While the theoretical bounds (positivity, unitarity and stability of the Higgs potential) as well as the electroweak STU pseudo-observables are available in the most general case, the Higgs and flavour observables are currently only implemented in the flavour aligned limiting case.
